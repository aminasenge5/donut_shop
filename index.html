Locations for Holy Moly Donut Shop: Holy Home-Slice on 131st and Halsey || Holy High-Mass on 140th and San Rafael || Holy High-Life on 12th and Burnside || Holy Half-Moon on 3rd and Taylor || Holy Hide-Away on Rocky Butte.

How to eat a Holy Moly Donut

  • Purchase from certified bakery (listed above).
  • Have napkins ready.
  • Have hot coffee or tea to enjoy with your Holy Moly Donut.
  • Find a comforable chair.
  • Say the Brachah Rishonah (special prayer).
  • Eat up!!!
